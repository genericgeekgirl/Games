In Volume - Scenes, Book - Wedding Ceremony:

Problem. You wrote 'After looking or examining or examining the player when Decision Making is happening'  , which seems to introduce a rule taking effect only 'looking or examining or examining the player when Decision Making is happening'. But this is a combination of actions which cannot be mixed. The only alternatives where 'or' is allowed are cases where a choice of actions is given but applying to the same objects in each case. (So 'taking or dropping the CD' is allowed, but 'dropping the CD or inserting the CD into the jewel box' is not, because the alternatives there would make different use of objects from each other.)

 See the manual: 7.8 > Rules applying to more than one action
