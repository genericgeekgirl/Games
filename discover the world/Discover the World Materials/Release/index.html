'Discover the World' is a lighthearted introduction to the world of Ur. The entire world and everything in it, including yourself, has been imagined by the Eleven Giants.

Discover the World was created with Inform and has IFID 0048FBAD-AB8E-4BD7-B9F6-043F664F71A9. To play a work like this one, you need an interpreter program: many are available, among them Zoom for Mac OS X and for Unix; Windows Frotz or Windows Glulxe for Windows. Or you can play without downloading anything by following the 'Play In-Browser' link, using the Quixe interpreter. You'll need to have Javascript enabled on your web browser.
